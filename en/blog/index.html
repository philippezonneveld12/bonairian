Welcome to the most comprehensive English-language guide to Bonaire — the Caribbean's premier diving destination. Honest reviews, practical tips, and everything you need to plan an unforgettable trip.

Bonaire is a special municipality of the Kingdom of the Netherlands in the Caribbean, world-famous for its crystal-clear water, healthy coral reefs, and rich biodiversity. Direct flights from US hubs (Houston, Atlanta, Newark, Miami) make it remarkably accessible, and the entire west coast is a protected marine park.
